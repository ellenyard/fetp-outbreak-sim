**Focus:** Conducting analytical studies to identify risk factors and confirm hypotheses.

## Your Tasks Today

## Analytical Approach
Design and analyze case-control or cohort study to:
- Identify significant risk factors (flood exposure, barefoot activity, occupational risks)
- Quantify strength of associations
- Assess dose-response relationships (duration of water contact)
- Evaluate protective factors (boots, gloves, prophylaxis)
